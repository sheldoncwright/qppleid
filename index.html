qppleid

qppleid.com was created to illustrate just how easy it is to be tricked into sharing your login information with a stranger. The problem is compounded if you have reused this password on other websites.

Never reuse passwords, use a password manager, pay close attention to details *like misspellings* and never enter your login information unless you're extremely confident that you're on an authentic website.
